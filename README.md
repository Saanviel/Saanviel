# Saanviel
## Overview
Saanviel is a service platform that connects professionals with businesses, offering expertise across multiple fields.  
Beyond general service delivery, it provides specialized support in **Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE)** management and consultation, helping organizations improve compliance, workplace safety, and sustainable practices.
## Features
- Professional service marketplace connecting experts with companies  
- Business networking and collaboration opportunities  
- Specialized **HSE consulting services**  
- Support for compliance, safety, and environmental management  
- Tools for professionals to showcase expertise and expand their network
   ## HSE Consultation Services
Saanviel offers tailored HSE solutions to help organizations:
- Strengthen workplace safety standards  
- Ensure compliance with international regulations  
- Implement sustainable environmental practices  
- Access expert consultation and training resources
